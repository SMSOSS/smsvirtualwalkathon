Participate in the Event

  1. Register for the event. A confirmation email with a registration number will be sent within two days once you have successfully registered.
  2. Invite relatives and friends to sponsor your walk. No minimum donation is required. All donations above HK$100 will be tax-deductible. Send a crossed cheque (payable to “St. Mark’s School Foundation Limited”) to St. Mark’s School Foundation Limited (c/o St. Mark’s School, 18 Oi Yin Street, Aldrich Bay, Shaukeiwan, Hong Kong) before 22 November 2021.
  3. Download any app which helps to track/collect your walking data (time, route, distance and number of footsteps).
  4. Plan your walk. Complete your walk anywhere and at any time between 22 November to 3 December 2021. Remember to take photos.
  5. Screencap the data and upload to the link provided.
  6. If you are unable to join us, a donation would be most gratefully appreciated.

If you join as a group, the maximum number of participants in a group is FOUR. Assign a group leader for registration and uploading the result.
