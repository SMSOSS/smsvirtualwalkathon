ABOUT THIS EVENT



incorporated on 28th April 2017
St.Mark's School Foundation Limited aims to support St.Mark's School for its advancement of education
by developing the potential , competence and skills of the students and staff
and to provide funds to the School for its development

Our first Virtual Fund-raising Walkathon will be held during 22 November to 3 December 2021
The event is open to students , alumni all firends of St.Mark's

Fund raised will go to St.Mark's School Foundation Limited
to be used by St.Mark's School in improving campus learning environment , enhancing students' development
and supporting students in need
especially those who are affected by the economic downturn caused by COVID-19.
